# Current-best Design decision evidence

- Status: `completed`
- Updated: `2026-07-23`
- Related design: [[docs/design/design-programs]], [[docs/design/coding-agent-optimization]], [[docs/design/agent-cli-contract]], and [[docs/design/experiment-workbench]].

## Outcome

Every valid Design iteration explains KEEP or REJECT with exact aggregate and per-case deltas against the current best it was challenging, and CLI Agents plus Studio operators see the same decisive case and reason without reconstructing search state across earlier iterations.

## Context

Design evaluates every candidate through a locked Benchmark whose case results report deltas from the fixed Benchmark baseline. The search decision has an additional boundary: a Benchmark-accepted candidate advances only when its aggregate score improves on the evolving current best. `scoreDeltaFromBest` records that aggregate comparison, but the iteration does not retain the corresponding per-case current-best scores or identify the limiting case.

The batch-formation experiment makes the gap concrete. Its driver batch wait falls from about 40 seconds to 7 seconds, and the candidate remains accepted against the original baseline, yet it is REJECTed at `-1.612859` versus iteration 3. The decisive lithography-interruption regression of about `-12.5969` is discoverable only by finding the previous KEEP and subtracting case scores manually. That is unnecessary cognitive work for both humans and Agents and makes the visible REJECT less explanatory than the underlying evidence.

## Scope

### In scope

- Record the exact previous-best score, candidate score, and delta for every locked Benchmark case on each valid Design iteration.
- Derive a deterministic decisive/limiting case and distinguish Benchmark-gate rejection, no improvement over current best, and KEEP.
- Include the same evidence in immutable Design Runs, progress completion events where useful, CLI human/JSON projections, and Studio live/reopened views.
- Regenerate current local Design evidence under the new result identity without compatibility adapters.

### Out of scope

- Changing Benchmark acceptance thresholds, Objective scoring, or the strict positive-improvement KEEP rule.
- Claiming that the worst case is the sole causal explanation for aggregate behavior.
- Adding prose generated by an LLM to the immutable decision contract.

## Findings and decisions

- 2026-07-23 — `BlueprintBenchmarkCaseResult.scoreDelta` remains correctly defined against the fixed Benchmark baseline. Design needs a separate explicitly named current-best comparison rather than changing that existing meaning.
- 2026-07-23 — `decisionEvidence` replaces the three loose iteration score fields. Its normalized contract owns one decision `basis`, aggregate comparison, ordered per-case comparisons, limiting-case id, and evaluator-owned gate reasons only when a locked gate fails.
- 2026-07-23 — Valid-run reopening must replay the current-best evaluation lineage from seed through each KEEP and reject any mismatch; structural shape checking alone is insufficient for immutable decision evidence.
